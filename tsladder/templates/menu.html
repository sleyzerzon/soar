**stats table**

Wimpy Wins: Wins achieved by reaching the maximum updates instead of the winning score.

Wimpy Win %: Percentage of total wins that were wimpy.

SNC Losses: Number of losses due to the agent being interrupted.

Average Points: Average number of points scored in a match

Average Ladder Points: Average number of ladder points scored per match:

Draw a match1 point

Achieve winning score

Rating: Tank rating (using Elo rating system).
