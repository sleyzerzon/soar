watch
Usage:	watch [level] [-n] [-a <switch>] [-b <switch>] [-c <switch>]
	[-d <switch>] [-D <switch>] [-i <switch>] [-j <switch>] [-l <detail>]
	[-L <switch>] [-p <switch>] [-P <switch>] [-r <switch>] [-u <switch>]
	[-w <switch>] [-W <detail>]
  -a, --aliases
  -b, --backtracing
  -c, --chunks
  -d, --decisions
  -D, --default-productions
  -i, --indifferent-selection
  -j, --justifications
  -l, --learning
  -L, --loading
  -n, --none
  -p, --phases
  -P, --productions
  -r, --preferences
  -u, --user-productions
  -w, --wmes
  -W, --wme-detail
  detail			Integer from 0-2:
				  * 0: Off/lowest detail
				  * 1: On/medium detail
				  * 2: On/highest detail
  level				Integer from 0-5:
  				  * 0: Watch nothing, equivalent to -n
  				  * 1: Watch decisions inclusive
  				  * 2: Watch phases and decisions
  				  * 3: Watch productions, phases, and 
  				       decisions
  				  * 4: Watch wmes, productions, phases,
  				       and decisions
  				  * 5: Watch preferences, wmes, productions,
  				       phases, and decisions
  switch			Switch argument:
  				  * 0: Turn watches for this option off
  				  * 1: Turn watches for this option on
